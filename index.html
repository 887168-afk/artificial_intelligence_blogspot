What is Artificial Intelligence?

AI is a technology that allows computers to recreate human attributes like creativity, learning, problem solving, decision making, and more. It uses algorithms, loops, data, and patterns to recreate the human-like behavior and to perform tasks. Some of these tasks include generating things like images or code, analyzing images or text, having a conversation, and more. The usual AIs that you hear today like ChatGPT or Copilot are a special type of AI called large language models. These types of AIs work through learning large amounts of data that can help them easily perform tasks.
