AI And Climate

Welcome!

This part of this website is about how AI affects our environment in today's world.

AI is a very powerful tool that doesn’t seem like it, but has a big impact on the environment. One big impact AI has on the environment is that it causes global warming on our earth. This happens because AI’s have hundreds of computers called servers, which help store information in the AI. These servers take up a lot of electricity to use. Some people who make these servers use fossil fuels to help make electricity for the AI to work. These fossil fuels when burned release carbon emissions. Since there is so much electricity needed for servers to work, many fossil fuels are burned. Due to all of this pollution in the air, the ozone layer gets damaged, and the earth gets heated up. This heating of the earth is called global warming. For example, if more servers were used, more fossil fuels would have to be burned and more global warming. If fewer servers were used, less fossil fuels would have to be burned and there would be less global warming. Another way AI is impacting our environment is through water depletion. This happens because these AI servers heat up a lot when being used. Since they get so hot, they need water cooling. The heat from the servers is too much, because the water evaporates much faster than usual, which requires more new freshwater. This issue is especially bad, as most companies make their servers use freshwater instead of using the freshwater for drinking. An example of water being depleted is prompts. For 100 words, about one water bottle worth of water is used up in each prompt. Due to the impact of AI today, thousands of water bottles are used up. A way that AI is also affecting the environment is through electronic waste. This happens because AI’s hardware is constantly updating, and old hardware is garbage. The old hardware actually contains hazardous substances like lead and mercury. Some examples of electronic waste are broken hardware, old phones, and damaged wires. Another impact of AI on the environment is its use of rare minerals. The reason why AI is using rare minerals is because its servers use complex hardware that need special minerals to work. As I’ve mentioned earlier, there are many servers that are for the AI to work. Due to the amount of servers needed, many of these rare minerals like copper, are being put more in demand and being used up even more. Because of this, many minerals like platinum, silver, and copper which are non-renewable, are being depleted. Some other minerals that are being depleted are aluminum, gold, tantalum, and more. When AI is being used, it has many impacts on the environment like global warming, water depletion, electronic waste, and mineral depletion.

What I think about AI affecting the environment is that its AI servers should be changed. The changes that I think should be made are that AI servers should switch to renewable energy sources, hardware should be planned so there isn’t any electronic waste, saltwater should be used for water cooling instead of drinking water or more fans instead of water cooling, and hardware companies should try to use less rare minerals if possible. The reason why I think the AI servers should be changed is because they are creating a bunch of problems today. Some of these problems are global warming, water depletion, electronic waste, and mineral depletion. These problems are bad because they are harming the environment by increasing heat in the earth, having less drinking water in the earth, having more pollution in the earth, and less non-renewable resources. Another reason why I think this is because if we have the changes I mentioned earlier, things would change for the better. If AI servers changed from fossil fuels to renewable energy like solar energy, we would have less pollution coming from the many AI servers there are. If AI servers use saltwater instead of drinking water or use more fans for cooling, we would have less drinking water being used or less water being used at all. If AI servers plan out their hardware for the future instead of making multiple types of hardware, there could be less electronic waste. If AI servers could possibly use other minerals that aren’t close to full depletion, we could preserve these rare minerals. An example on how AI servers can create less problems is the supplier’s money. In order to pay for the expensive hardware, the company’s money could easily allow them to do many things. Some of these things are going from non-renewable energy sources to renewable energy sources, buying more fans for the servers, or planning their hardware out. The way I think about AI and its effects on the environment is that there are too many problems with AI servers, and they should be changed.
