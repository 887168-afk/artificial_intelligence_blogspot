AI Is Being Misused

AI misuse isn’t very good and can give horrible side effects. There are many ways AI can be used wrong, which then lead to bad consequences. These ways can be using AI when you’re not supposed to, straight-out copy and pasting the answer AI gives, or using AI all the time for everything. Due to this kind of use of AI, horrible side effects like less critical thinking, using AI too much, and jobs or marks being lost, which makes you perform much worse. Critical thinking can be lost through daily AI use. Using AI all the time makes you more reliant on AI, and makes using AI a habit. Jobs can be lost due to employees relying and using AI, and getting caught. Students can also lose marks, due to use of AI. Artificial intelligence being misused is a bad thing, and especially for the side effects and what it can do to you.
